Future & career prospects with Media Design

Career opportunities

Fontys Media Design offers many career opportunities not only in the sphere of the Media. The main point of the course is to educate future front-end developers and UX/UI researchers. These carreers are widely demanded in the IT sector and are highly valued.

A Front-end developer is a software engineer who specializes in the development of the front-end of a website or application. The front-end of a website or application is the part that users interact with, and it is responsible for the look and feel of the site or app. Front-end developers typically use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to build the user interface of a website or application, and they work closely with designers and back-end developers to ensure that the final product is functional, visually appealing, and easy to use.

Front-end development wikipedia page

A UX (User Experience) designer is a professional who focuses on the design of user experiences for products and services, with the goal of making them easy, efficient, and enjoyable to use. A UX designer might work on the design of websites, mobile apps, software applications, or other types of products, and might be involved in various aspects of the design process, including research, prototyping, and testing.

User Experience wikipedia page

A UI (User Interface) designer is a professional who focuses on the design of the visual elements of a product's user interface, such as its layout, color scheme, and typography. A UI designer might work on the design of websites, mobile apps, software applications, or other types of products, and might be involved in various aspects of the design process, including wireframing, prototyping, and testing.

User Interface wikipedia page

In general, the goal of a UX/UI designer/researcher is to create products that are intuitive, efficient, and enjoyable for users, while also meeting the business goals of the organization.

The Fontys ICT Media Design Bachelors degree is also not limited to job positions such as:

Digital Marketeer

A digital marketeer is a professional who specializes in promoting and marketing products or services through digital channels such as social media, email, websites, and mobile apps. Digital marketeers may also be responsible for managing the online presence of a company or organization.

SEO/SEA Specialist

An SEO/SEA specialist is a professional who specializes in improving the visibility of a website or online content in search engines like Google, Bing, and Yahoo. SEO stands for "search engine optimization," and it involves using a variety of tactics and strategies to improve a website's ranking in search engine results pages (SERPs).

Interaction designer

An interaction designer is a professional who specializes in designing the way that people interact with products, systems, or services. This can include things like designing the user interface (UI) of a website or mobile app, creating prototypes or mockups of product features, and conducting user research to understand the needs and behaviors of the people who will be using the product.

Masters degree opportunities with Media design

If you have completed a bachelor's degree in media design from Fontys University of Applied Sciences or a similar program, you may be able to pursue a master's degree in a related field. Some potential options might include a Master of Fine Arts (MFA) in design, a Master of Arts (MA) in media studies, or a Master of Science (MSc) in interactive media. Other possible areas of study could include animation, game design, web development, or digital media. Some master's programs may require you to have a specific undergraduate degree or specific coursework as a prerequisite, so you should check the requirements of the programs you are interested in to ensure that you are eligible to apply.
