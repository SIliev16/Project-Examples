Fontys R10 Campus

Video lab

At Fontys R10 building there is a fully equipped video lab, complete with all the necessary equipment and resources for creating and editing professional-quality video content. The lab is equipped with a variety of cameras, monitors, and lighting equipment, as well as a green screen wall that can be used for special effects and compositing. The video lab is a valuable resource for students and staff at Fontys who are interested in exploring the creative possibilities of video production and post-production.

Audio Lab

At Fontys R10 building there is a fully equipped audio room that is designed for the recording, editing, and mixing of audio content. The space is outfitted with soundproofing and acoustic treatment to minimize external noise and enhance the quality of the audio, as well as specialized equipment like microphones, audio interfaces, and mixing consoles. This audio room is suitable for a wide range of audio recording and production activities, such as podcasts, songs, and sound effects.

Meeting rooms

In the R10 building at Fontys, there are several special meeting rooms that are scattered throughout the building. These rooms are designed to accommodate up to 8 people and are equipped with tables, chairs, and monitors. In addition, they are soundproofed to ensure a quiet and private environment for meetings and discussions. These meeting rooms are a valuable resource for students, staff, and other members of the Fontys community who need a quiet and well-equipped space to work or collaborate.

Private rooms

In addition to the larger meeting rooms, the R10 building at Fontys University of Applied Sciences in Eindhoven also has a number of smaller, private rooms that are suitable for smaller groups of no more than 4 people. These rooms are soundproofed to provide a quiet and secluded space for focused work or private conversations. They are also designed to be cozy and comfortable, making them a good option for anyone who wants to retreat to a more peaceful setting. These private rooms are located throughout the building and are a useful resource for students, staff, and other members of the Fontys community who need a quiet and intimate space to work or meet.

ICT Student Service Desk (ISSD)

At the R10 building at Fontys, there is a dedicated ICT Student Service Desk, or ISSD, that serves as the first point of contact for students and staff with questions or issues related to ICT (information and communication technology) in their studies or work. The ISSD is committed to helping you resolve any ICT-related problems as quickly as possible.
In addition to providing support and assistance, the ISSD also offers a range of equipment and resources that can be borrowed by students and staff for use in their studies or work. This includes items such as laptops, cameras, robots, sensors, microcontrollers, and office equipment. If you need a specific item or have left something at home, the ISSD may be able to provide a loaner for you to use.

Comfort

The R10 building at Fontys University of Applied Sciences in Eindhoven is full of amenities and features that are meant to make students feel comfortable and at home. It has a variety of spaces and areas that are ideal for relaxation and socializing, such as break areas, lounges, open spaces, and terraces. These are great places to take a break, unwind, or hang out with friends.

In addition to these more leisurely areas, the R10 building also has a number of recreational facilities that are available for students to enjoy. These include table tennis, table football, arcade games, and a billiard table, all of which are free to use. There are also equipment such as microwaves, coffee machines, vending machines, and fridges that are available for everyone to use. These amenities are meant to help students feel at home and make the most of their time at Fontys.
